Harry Potter

The protagonist, known as "The Boy Who Lived," Harry is a courageous young wizard marked by a lightning bolt scar. He is determined to stop Lord Voldemort and uncover the truth about his parents' death.

Hermione Granger

A brilliant and resourceful witch, Hermione is one of Harry's closest friends. She is known for her intelligence, hard work, and loyalty, often saving the day with her quick thinking.

Ron Weasley

Harry's loyal best friend, Ron is a kind-hearted, brave, and somewhat clumsy wizard. He comes from a large, loving family and often provides comic relief during intense moments.

Lord Voldemort

The dark wizard and main antagonist, Voldemort seeks immortality and domination over the wizarding world. He is feared by all and is known for his ruthless, manipulative nature.

Draco Malfoy

A student from a wealthy and pure-blood family, Draco often acts as Harry's rival at Hogwarts. Though initially antagonistic, his character evolves over time, revealing his inner conflicts..

Hagrid

The gentle giant and Hogwarts gamekeeper, Hagrid is a protector of magical creatures and one of Harry's first friends in the wizarding world. His warm heart and loyalty make him a beloved character
